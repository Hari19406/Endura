# Fatigue Model (v1)

## Inputs
- RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion)
- Sleep quality
- Previous training load
- Rest days

## Output
Fatigue Score (0–100)

## Logic (Basic)
- High RPE + low sleep → increase fatigue
- Rest day → reduce fatigue
- Consecutive hard workouts → spike fatigue

## Purpose
This score will decide:
- Workout intensity
- Recovery days
- Risk of overtraining
